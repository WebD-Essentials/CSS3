Once upon a time there was a Chinese farmer whose horse ran away. That evening, all of his neighbors came
around to commiserate. They said, “We are so sorry to hear your horse has run away. This is most
unfortunate.” The farmer said, “Maybe.” The next day the horse came back bringing seven wild horses with it,
and in the evening everybody came back and said, “Oh, isn't that lucky. What a great turn of events. You now
have eight horses!” The farmer again said, “Maybe.”
